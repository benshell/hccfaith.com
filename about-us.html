About Us

Vision, Mission and Core Beliefs

Vision Statement: To become a church that wins and connects the communities of Los Angeles County to Christ through Care Groups.

Mission Statement: To glorify God by bringing people to Christ, cultivating Christian maturity and developing the body of Christ for ministry and her mission to the world through Care Groups.

Slogan: Harvest Christian Center - “A Place Where Everyone is Connected”

Six Core Values

Salvation: We share the gospel of Jesus Christ with the lost. (Mark 16:15-18, I Corinthians 15:1-6)

Saluting God: We salute God through prayer, praise and worship. (James 5:16-17, Mark 1:35, Matthew 21:22, Psalms 150:1-6, John 4:23-24)

Spiritual Growth: We develop believers into spiritual maturity through biblical teaching, training and modeling a Godly lifestyle. (John 8:31-32, II Peter 3:18, I Timothy 4:7, II Timothy 2:15)

Small Care Groups: We participate in small care groups which foster love, fellowship, support and accountability while developing disciples. (Acts 2:46-47, Acts 5:42)

Serving Opportunities: We look for opportunities to serve those in need. (Matthew 20:25-28, II Timothy 2:24-26, Galatians 5:13-14)

Stewardship: We acknowledge that all that we are and have belong to God and that God commands us to be responsible mangers of our resources.
(I Corinthians 4:2, Luke 16:1-8, Luke 12:41-44)
